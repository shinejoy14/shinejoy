ABOUT US

Mariam Dnewme

Owner

Mariam is a psychiatric nurse, motivational speaker, entrepreneur and a lifestyle coach. She is a mother and a wife. Mariam had been passionate about healthy living and fitness since she was young. Despite her love for fitness, Mariam was one of the individuals that was overweight as a result of an unhealthy lifestyle, she experienced many sicknesses associated with being overweight, this include fatigue/tiredness, blur vision, leg pain, withdrawal from normal activities. Also this affects her mental health as she was always irritable, and aggressive. Mariam decided to take charge of her health by doing research on how to lose weight and healthy lifestyle. Since then, Mariam has lost 15kg of her excess weight, and all the sickness associated with being overweight has been reversed without medication or surgery. Mariam has since then enjoyed good physical and mental health.

She has been involved in various fitness programmes and challenges such as 5k parkrun, mini marathons, Ray Dacy marathon. Mariam is a member of her local gym where she participates in the various fitness classes such as ab and ass, spin, DanceHitt and so on. All this has helped to keep her in shape and fit both physically and mentally. Mariam has achieved various certificates in coaching,nutrition and fitness. Mariam is the CEO of Dnewme lifestyle coach.

Dnewme lifestyle coaching philosophy

Denewme has been established to meets the needs of both men and women that are not confident in their body shape. Men and Women who have no motivation to change their lifestyle in regards to exercise, staying fits and eating the right food. Dnewme is available to help fill the gap through fun fitness and exercise with the incorporation of healthy nutrition..

Mission

Dnewme offers a nutrition and fitness programme to people who are willing to shed off some of their weight and maintain a healthy lifestyle which helps to live a quality of life. Our programs are designed to respect the privacy and opinion of our clients. Dnewme is keen to help both men and women achieve and maintain a healthy lifestyle through fitness and nutrition. Dnewme is committed to respect our clients.
