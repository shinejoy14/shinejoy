Quinoa veggies and shrimp.

Healthy and tasty.

Ingredients

Quinoa 100 grams Shrimp 300 gram Mixed pepper 100 grams Chopped carrots 50gram Chopped onions 1 medium Chopped tomatoes 2 whole chopped Cayenne pepper 1 Olive oil 2 tbsp Pinch of salt and pepper Chopped Garlic and ginger

Direction

Shrimp veggies Heat oil in a pan Add chopped carrot, onions,ginger, garlic, cayane pepper and mixed pepper. Stir fry for 3 minute then add chopped tomatoes. Add shrimp and bring to simmer for 10 minutes Add salt and pepper to taste. Take pan off from heat. Cooking Quoina Add 100 grams of quinoa in a bowl of water. Wash until water is clean and clear. Pour washed quinoa in a pan and add Â½ cup of water. Add salt to taste Bring to boil for 10 minutes until soft and fluffy. Turn off heat and let quinoa settled. Serve cooked quinoa and veggies shrimp in a plate. Enjoy it.
